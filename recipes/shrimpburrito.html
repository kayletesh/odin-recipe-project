Shrimp Burrito

Simple, Tasty Shrimp Burritos

Shrimp burritos is one of my favorite things to make. Super simple, tasty, and low cost.

Ingredients

  • 5-8 medium sized shrimp
  • 1 tortilla
  • shredded cheese
  • sour cream
  • butter
  • salt
  • pepper
  • garlic

Steps

  1. Peel and devein your shrimp
  2. Dry the shrimp, then add salt, pepper, and garlic.
  3. Heat stovetop to medium heat
  4. Place pan on burner
  5. Add butter and lemon to the pan
  6. Cook shrimp for 3 minutes each side, or until each side is pink
  7. Remove shrimp from the pan and reduce heat to medium low
  8. Place tortilla in pan
  9. Add shrimp and cheese back onto the tortilla as it is cooking
  10. This step can take less than a minute, so check the bottom of the tortilla often
  11. remove tortilla from pan
  12. Add your cold toppings and sour cream
  13. ENJOY!!
